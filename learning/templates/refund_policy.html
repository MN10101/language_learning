Refund Policy

We offer a refund within 14 days of purchase if the course has not been started. Once a course has been accessed or started, refunds are no longer available.

1. Refund Eligibility

If you are not satisfied with your purchase and the course has not been started, you can request a refund within 14 days.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@example.com with your order details and the reason for your refund request.

3. Processing Refunds

Once your refund request is approved, we will process the refund to your original payment method within 7 business days.

If you have any questions regarding our refund policy, please contact us at support@example.com.
